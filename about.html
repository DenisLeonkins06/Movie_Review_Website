About FlickReview

What is FlickReview?

FlickReview is a simple movie review platform where users can browse movies, leave ratings, and share their opinions about films they’ve watched.

How it works

• Browse featured movies on the homepage
• Add your own review with a rating out of 10
• View what other users think
• Remove reviews if needed

Tech Stack

This project is built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Supabase as the backend database.

Project Goal

The goal of FlickReview is to demonstrate how a full-stack CRUD application works, including creating, reading, and deleting data in real time.
